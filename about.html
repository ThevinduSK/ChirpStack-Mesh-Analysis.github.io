About

I'm a 3rd-year Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering undergraduate at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. I'm passionate about telecommunication protocols, network architecture, and system design.

This research project — a comprehensive evaluation of the ChirpStack Gateway Mesh protocol — involved reverse-engineering the Rust-based mesh firmware, building a full-stack Python measurement framework, and conducting extensive experiments to characterise performance, duty cycle behaviour, and protocol limitations across multiple spreading factor configurations.
